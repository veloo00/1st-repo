My Top 3 Favorite Movies

1. The Fast and The Furious 3: Tokyo Drift (2006)

*Tokyo Drift* is a film about an American teenager Sean Boswell being sent to Japan to live with his father to avoid jail time in the US for street racing. He eventually starts to race in Japan as well, becoming the new drift king after racing with Takashi, the former Drift King.

  • Sean Boswell
  • Neela
  • Takashi

2. F1: The Movie (2025)

*F1* is an American sports film starring Brad Pitt and Damson Idris. Brad Pitt plays character Sonny Hayes, a racing veteran coming back to the sport to mentor a young prodigy in a struggling team, Joshua Pearce, who is played by Damson Idris.

  • Sonny Hayes
  • Joshua Pearce
  • Lewis Hamilton

3. Avengers: Endgame (2019)

*Avengers: Endgame* is a movie following the last few avengers as they try to reverse the universe-altering devastation caused by Thanos.

  • Iron Man
  • Cap America
  • Thanos
